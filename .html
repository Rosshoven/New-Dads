NEW DADS

of Naperville




NEW DADS is a neighborhood club decidated to

supporting men during their transition into Fatherhood.

A lot of emotions come up when you are becoming a Dad.

NEW DADS wants you to know you are not alone with those feelings.

We have a monthly e-newsletter and meet-up once a month.

Our last meet-up in May was at Lanes Bowling Alley. We had a great time bowling, chatting, and eating wings. Check out some of the pics.

Our next meet-up will be @ Wizard Park on June 18th 3:00-5:00pm. We'll have talk-time, then hang out at the BBQ.

Bring a disc or a ball to throw around if you're feeling active. Or just sit and enjoy some sun and lemonade.
